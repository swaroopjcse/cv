I am a Senior Lecturer in the Department of \cse{} at \osu. My interest is in a
range of topics in Engineering Education and Software Engineering, including but
not limited to Computing Education, Engineering Education, Global Engineering
Education, Service Learning, Software Engineering, and Programming Languages. I
have taught 2,506 credit hours to 724 students in 8 different core CS courses
between Spring 2013 and Fall 2018 semesters. In the process, I have supervised 5
graduate and 22 undergraduate teaching assistants. I have published papers in
peer-reviewed conferences on engineering education. I serve as an associate
editor of the \jeet{} and the secretary-treasurer of the Computers in Education
Division of the \asee.
